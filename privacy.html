Our Commitment to Your Privacy

What Information Do We Collect?

At Babcock University Fruit Store, we value your privacy and are committed to protecting your personal information. The data we collect is limited to what is necessary to serve you better and ensure a smooth shopping experience. This includes:

  • Your name and contact details
  • Order preferences and history
  • Payment information (we accept cash & transfer)
  • Communication preferences (e.g., email updates)

How Do We Use Your Information?

We use the information you provide to:

  • Process your orders and payments
  • Communicate with you about our products and services
  • Continuously improve our website and customer service
  • Send you marketing updates (but only with your consent)

We will never share, sell, or misuse your personal data. Your trust is of the utmost importance to us.

Protecting Your Information

We take the security of your personal information very seriously.Which includes encrypted connections, access controls, and regular security audits.

Your Privacy Rights

You have the right to access, update, or delete the information we have on file for you. If you have any questions or concerns about our privacy practices, please don't hesitate to reach out to our team.
