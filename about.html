About

Our Story

We are campus based fruit store dedicated to providing fresh and highly nutricious fruits to the students and staffs. It was founded through the university agriculture department and entrepreneur. We provide you with fresh fruits which is highly affordable and we also promote healthy eating and balanced diet.

We work directly with local farmers to ensure that every piece of fruit meets your high standards for quality and freshness.

Our Commitment

At Babcock University Fruit Vendor, we are passionate about providing our customers with the best possible fruit-buying experience. We take pride in our extensive selection of seasonal and locally-sourced, all of which are hand-picked for their exceptional taste and freshness.

Our team of dedicated professionals works tirelessly to ensure that our produce is stored and displayed in optimal conditions, so you can enjoy the peak flavor and texture of each fruit.

Sustainable Practices

We are committed to sustainable and ethical business practices. We source our fruits from growers who use environmentally-friendly farming methods, and we strive to minimize waste throughout our supply chain.

Additionally, we actively support local and regional agricultural initiatives, helping to strengthen our community's food system and promote a thriving local economy.
